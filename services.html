  • Favicon
  • Custom 404 page
  • Mobile and iPad/tablet responsive design

  • Full site optimisation for social media sharing
  • Basic customised Instagram links page (to replace Linktree, Linkin.bio etc)
  • Access to our content management system, CloudCannon, where you can make direct edits to your site
