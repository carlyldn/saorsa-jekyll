my story

Hello, I'm Carly and I'm the owner and one-woman-show behind Saorsa Studio.

‘Saorsa’ means ‘freedom’ in Scottish Gaelic. It embodies a lot of what our brand is all about, having the freedom to design and build totally custom coded online spaces. It also represents the decision to quit my 9-5 in . In the midst of the global pandemic, I was approaching 6 years working in the same office role and I wanted to escape the eat, sleep, work, repeat routine.

Despite the security of my job in those — *unprecedented times* — I went in search of a more meaningful and creative role. I took a leap of faith and joined a web development course after having dabbled in it a little at university. I immediately fell in love with it and feel as though I have found my purpose - and an immense sense of freedom along with it.

Being able to design, code and build a site from the ground up is truly so rewarding. There's also no better feeling than being a small part of helping your business thrive!

If you'd like to chat over a virtual (or real life!) coffee to discuss a project, I'd love to hear from you.
