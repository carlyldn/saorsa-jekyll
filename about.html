What does custom code mean?

Custom code means that we build our websites from the ground up. We don't use any generic templates or drag and drop editors to create our sites, everything is coded on a project to project basis. This gives us an immense amount of flexibility around how our websites are designed which helps you stand out from the crowd.

Not only is this great from a design point of view, but having a totally custom coded website is a benefit for SO many reasons. They're fast, incredibly secure and you'll be able to update and edit them yourself with our integrated content management system. Don't worry, you won't need to know a single line of code!

Our websites are easy to maintain too, completely mobile responsive - and of course - designed completely with your brand aesthetic in mind.

What are the benefits of a custom coded website?

Fast loading

Users are extremely impatient when waiting for a website to load, so every second counts! We can get into the technical nitty gritty if you're interested in learning more, but the short version is that we use the latest in static website technology so that your site loads instantly. Not only that, but our sites are coded thoughtfully and intentionally ensuring that there is no unnecessary code to slow down your load time.

Incredibly secure
On top of static sites being quick to load, they're also much less vulnerable to attacks. Static sites don't interact with a database, meaning there are FAR less vulnerabilities for potential hackers to exploit. We also use the latest technology to ensure that your site remains secure, leaving you with one less thing thing to worry about.
Built with SEO best practice in mind
We add practical SEO functionality to your site to help you rank in Google searches. You can add and adapt custom meta titles and keywords for each page. We use coding best practice to boost your SEO ranking along with ensuring your images are optimised and your site loads quickly. We also work with a Google Analytics specialist who will ensure your website is set up for tracking and can even run and manage Google Ads for you as an optional extra.
Optimised for social media
We make sure that your whole website is optimised for when someone shares a page or blog post to social media. This is something that can often be overlooked, and we ensure there is a relevant description and thumbnail so that when people see your website on socials, they'll be more likely to click through to visit your website and read more.
Device responsive
Mobile accounts for 50% of web traffic worldwide, so it's important that your website functions just as beautifully on mobile as it does on a desktop. We make sure that your website is just as user-friendly, and looks just as beautiful on mobile as it does on the web. We also tailor our sites for various other device sizes including tablets and notebooks.
Flexible
The great thing about having a custom coded site is that they're so flexible. Maybe you need a single page site for your business now, but in a year you might want to add more pages or a blog. We're here to support you with your website in the present and in the future as your business grows and adapts! We also give you access to our amazing content management system so that you can make instant changes to imagery and copy yourself.
