What is custom code?

Custom code means that we build our websites from the ground up. We don't use any generic templates or drag and drop editors to create our sites, everything is hand coded. This gives us an immense amount of flexibility around how our websites are designed which helps you stand out from the crowd.

Not only is this great from a design point of view, but there are also a number of other benefits to having a custom coded website. They're fast, incredibly secure and you'll be able to update and edit them yourself with our integrated content management system. Don't worry, you won't need to know a single line of code!

Our websites are easy to maintain too, completely mobile responsive - and of course - designed completely with your brand aesthetic in mind.

My story

Hello, I'm Carly and I'm the owner and one-woman-show behind Saorsa Studio.

Saorsa Studio was born during the global pandemic after I was approaching 6 years working in the same office job. I longed to escape the eat, sleep, work, repeat routine. Despite the security of my job in those — *unprecedented times* — I decided that it was time for me to go in search of a more meaningful and creative role.

This is where the name ‘Saorsa’ came from, it means ‘freedom’ in Scottish Gaelic. It represents the decision to quit my 9-5 and follow my dreams. It also felt fitting given that I also have the freedom to design and build totally custom coded online spaces for you!

I took a leap of faith and joined a web development course after having dabbled in it a little at university. I instantly knew that this would be a career path I would love, and I feel as though I have found my purpose - and an immense sense of freedom along with it.

There's no better feeling for me than being a small part of helping your business thrive. If you'd like to chat over a virtual (or real life!) coffee to discuss a project, I'd love to hear from you.
