Our Approach

Kind. Personal. Collaborative.

We're proud to be a small independent studio. We work closely with you to ensure that every step we take is aligned with your vision.

Saorsa [sir-sha] means freedom in Scottish Gaelic. We're dreamers and explorers, always searching for new creative ways to bring your brand to life.

Meet the Founder

Hello, I'm Carly and I'm the owner and one-woman-show behind Saorsa Studio.

The idea of Saorsa Studio was born after six years of the eat, sleep, work, repeat routine. I left office life behind and feel as though I have found my purpose in web development. I love that every day brings new design and tech challenges along with opportunities to work with other likeminded business owners!

There's no better feeling for me than being a small part of helping your business thrive. If you'd like to chat over a virtual or in person coffee to discuss a project, I'd love to hear from you.
