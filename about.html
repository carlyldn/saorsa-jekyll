Saorsa [sir-sha] means freedom in Scottish Gaelic. We're all about chasing your dreams, pursuing your wildest fantasies and making waves in business.

My story

Hello, I'm Carly and I'm the owner and one-woman-show behind Saorsa Studio.

The idea of Saorsa Studio was born after I was approaching 6 years working in the same office job. I longed for a sense of freedom and to escape the eat, sleep, work, repeat routine. I left office life behind and feel as though I have found my purpose in web development. I love that every day brings new design and tech challenges along with opportunities to work with other likeminded business owners!

There's no better feeling for me than being a small part of helping your business thrive. If you'd like to chat over a virtual or in person coffee to discuss a project, I'd love to hear from you.
